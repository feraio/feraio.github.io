ABOUT ME

Sports and nature lover, I use my creativity and happiness to try to be a superhero to my young sisters and my three dogs.

I am a 22-years-old Designer from Brazil, a beautiful and unique country that hosts multiple cultures and awesome creative minds. I'm always looking for a great opportunity to learn or work with interesting people from different places around the world.

EDUCATION

I started study Design at MICROCAMP in Sao Paulo in early 2008, finishing in late 2009 a technical course degree as Web Master, focused on Web Design and Web Development.

In 2012 I started a Bachelor Degree in Design at Faculdade Paulista de Artes, where I discovered my passion for digital media interfaces, finishing the course in late 2015.

Over the last years I've also done other courses related to Design, some of the latest was:

Usability Course @ Mergo - iMasters Lab - São Paulo, Brazil
Human Computer Interaction @  University of California, San Diego - US
Design Portfolio @ SENAC - São Paulo, Brazil

SKILLS

AND MORE
